Databases config

Databases Config

Where you want to store your new site's data ?

Enter the server address of your database. For local servers, it's generally localhost or 127.0.0.1

Enter the username that you use to connect to your database's server. For local servers, it's generally root

Enter the password of the username that you use to connect to your database's server. For local servers, it's generally nothing

Enter the name of the database that you want to use for your site.

Enter the text to add at the beginning of all tables's name. It's useful when you want to install more than one copy of CMS on the same database

Fields with a * are required !

Site config

Site Config

Give us some informations about your new site ! You will be able to change these informations after.

Enter the name of your new site. Try to find a name who reflect the content of the site.

Enter the description of your new site. This information is not really important now, but it's highly recommended to define her.

Choose if you need that search engines index your new site. We recommend you to choose yes after you have posted more than 3 contents.

Fields with a * are required !

Account config

Account Config

Create the admin account that you will use to manage your site

Enter the username that you will use for your admin account.

Enter the password of the username entered. It's recommended that you use long password, with numbers, specials characters (@, #, $, %, &) and spaces.

Enter the email address that you will use for send newsletters to your users.

Fields with a * are required !
